History

Born in Sousa - PB, moved out to Campina Grande in 2008 where studied high school. In 2012 he began college, bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the Federal University of Campina Grande. After two years of college he applied to an exchange program where he had the opportunity of study at San Francisco State University for two semesters. At the same year that he went to San Francisco, he started the two hobbies that are his favorites now, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and dance Brazilian Zouk. While he was in the United States(US) he also lived in Los Angeles(LA) during his summer internship, where he worked in a startup called Project Pals. While in LA started to training Zouk with professional dancers and also made part of the Zouk community, where he helped the development of the community through the organization of events, speacially in Orange County. After months living the american dream, he had to go back home to finish his graduation, but he was changed, he couldn't live without Zouk anymore, that's why he and his friend Rinaldo decided to start, in 2015, the CG Zouk Project, a iniciative to develop the zouk community in Campina Grande. Since them he is teaching dance classes while finishes graduation.

Working on it...
